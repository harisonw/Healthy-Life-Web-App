Welcome to Healthy Life!

Healthy Life is a website dedicated to helping you live a healthier life. We have a wide range of articles on a variety of topics to help you achieve your goals. We also have a community of people who are also trying to live a healthier life and share their Heathier Life Hacks on the HL Hacks page.

Recommended Actions to Get Started on HL:
  1. Go to the profile page and fill out your Heath Info, this will help us to recommend relevant articles to you in the Top 3 Recommended Articles section on the home page.
  2. Go to the connections page and link with your Apple Watch or Fitbit to help us recommend even better articles to you in the Top 3 Recommended Articles section on the home page.
  3. Go to the profile page and Setup 2FA to secure your account
  4. Go to the Categories page and read articles from the categories that interest you.
  5. Go to the HL Hacks page and read HL Hacks from the community.
  6. Go to the HL Hacks page and share your first Heathier Life Hack with the community.
